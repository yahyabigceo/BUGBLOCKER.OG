Flying Insect Control

Problems with swarming bees and flies?

Our pest control team delivers straightforward and effective solutions for your infestation problems. To maintain the overall sanitation and structure of your home and commercial spaces, we conduct services to eliminate the following pests:

Bees & Wasps

Bees are harmless unless they have been bothered. You can reach out to us for inspection if you are suspecting a bee or wasp colony nearby or your house is being infested. If you have identified the location of the beehive, we are here to help you safely relocate it in a more distant place.

Flies

Flies are common pests in both suburban homes and residential spaces. You can suspect an infestation if there is a regular sighting of flies in large numbers. Maggots, or flies in the larval stage, are commonly present in disposal areas or nearby food. Our scenario-specific solutions for fly control include non-toxic insecticides, fly bait installation, and aerosol application.

Mosquitoes & Midges

Aside from bites, one quick way to identify mozzies in your area is the presence of larvae on stagnant waters. Mosquitoes are attracted to freshly watered lawns or grassy areas, while midges dwell on shady, humid spots at dawn and dusk. You can reach out and talk to our specialists to discuss suitable measures for exterminating mosquitoes and midges in your area.
