Bird & Rodent Control

Are there pests stealing your food or ravaging your house at night?

Our company is insured to carry out pest management methods including bird, rodent and possum control using safe and humane methods. Here are some basic insights to help you learn more about these unwanted dwellersâ€™ characteristics and behavior:

Birds

Birds tend to dwell on residential and commercial building roofs if they know that a regular food source is nearby. This will result in frequent bird droppings and eventual damage to your roofs and gutters. Our bird control expert will install bird-proofing nets all over your roof to prevent them from landing and inhabiting there.

Rodents

To find out if you have rats or mice in your place, you can use a flashlight to examine dark areas such as cupboards or closets. Gnawed holes and scratch marks are two of the sure signs that they are present. Our team of rat control experts performs a thorough inspection to figure out how big the rodent problem is, and what is the best method of control that will suit you.

Possums

Possums are largely nocturnal and scavenge for food at night. These tree-dwelling marsupials are protected under the Wildlife Act 1975, which requires a safe and humane capture and control method. To execute this, we set up motion sensor spotlights and use harm-free live traps to capture them.
